# intel-corporate-responsibility-case-study
A redesigned visualization of Intel’s Corporate Responsibility Report highlighting key achievements in renewable energy, water stewardship, STEM impact, and workforce inclusion. Includes a polished slide presentation and a clear, accessible summary of Intel’s sustainability and social impact progress.

This work was completed as part of my internship program experience in the Global Career Accelerator and reflects my ability to translate complex corporate data into compelling, human-centered visual communication.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview  
Intel publishes an annual Corporate Responsibility Report to communicate its global efforts around sustainability, renewable energy, social impact, workforce diversity, and responsible innovation.  
  
My goal in this project was to transform these insights into a **clear, digestible, and strategic visual narrative** suitable for presentation, recruiting contexts, and stakeholder discussions.

This redesigned slide deck highlights Intel’s most impactful areas of progress through refined copy, improved structure, and professional visual clarity.

---

## 🎯 Project Goals  
- Extract the most meaningful insights from Intel’s corporate responsibility achievements  
- Rewrite the content into clear, polished, presentation‑ready headlines  
- Enhance visual hierarchy for more professional and compelling storytelling  
- Communicate progress in sustainability, STEM advancement, and DEI with clarity and impact  
- Practice turning dense corporate reporting into high-level visual summaries

---

## 📊 Key Insights Highlighted in the Deck  

### **1. Global Renewable Energy Leadership**  
Intel’s transition to **100% renewable electricity** across major global regions demonstrates its long‑term commitment to sustainable energy systems.  

### **2. Water Stewardship Success**  
Intel reached **net positive water**, restoring more water than it consumed — an essential milestone for environmental responsibility.  

### **3. STEM Education Impact**  
Intel reached **1.5 million students globally** through STEM programs, supporting the next generation of innovators.  

### **4. Workforce Inclusion & Internships**  
With **8,642 interns worldwide** and strong full-time conversion rates, Intel continues to grow a diverse and sustainable talent pipeline.  

### **5. Leadership Representation**  
Women’s representation on Intel’s Board increased from **20% to over 40%**, reflecting measurable progress in leadership diversity.
